Our History

TechCon was established in 2010 as a grassroots effort to bring together tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most respected technology conferences worldwide.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to spark innovation, promote tech education, and build bridges between developers, creators, and innovators from around the world.

Notable Past Speakers

Grace Codewell – Cybersecurity expert and 2021 keynote speaker, known for her work in data privacy reform.

Max Byte – AI researcher and open-source advocate. He spoke on ethical AI practices in 2023.
